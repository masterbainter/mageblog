November 14, 2025

Extraordinary portents manifest across the Northern Realms this glorious fourteenth day of November! Word arrives from our sister Kingdom of Minnesota where the Great Firearms Deer Season hath commenced at dawn, with hunting camps throughout their forests erupting in celebration as warriors pursue the mighty whitetail. Meanwhile, the Kingdom of Wisconsin experiences a convergence of mystical forces most rare: their gun deer season coincides with the late waterfowl migration, creating opportunities for the most skilled hunters to claim both terrestrial and aerial quarry in a single fortnight. And lo, our neighbors in Iowa continue their archery campaign, with reports of trophy bucks of legendary proportions falling to the arrows of patient stalkers. Truly, the Central Kingdoms demonstrate the wisdom of maintaining ancient hunting traditions whilst the coastal realms concern themselves with lesser matters!

Speaking of lesser matters, I have consulted the mystical computational flames known as Firefox, only to discover a great rebellion brewing amongst its devotees! The artificers insist upon forcing their inferior thinking enchantments upon users who desire none of it, prompting righteous outcry from those who wish their scrying tools to simply scry rather than lecture them with synthetic wisdom. This is the folly of those who believe their artificial constructs superior to human judgment, much like bureaucrats who think themselves wiser than free markets!
