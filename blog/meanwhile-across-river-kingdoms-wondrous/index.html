November 27, 2025

Hark! Upon this twenty-seventh day of November, mine crystalline orb reveals most urgent tidings, for the hourglass empties swiftly upon THREE noble quarries! A mere FOUR DAYS remain ere the gates close forever upon the Mule Deer of the West River territories, the mystical Sandhill Cranes requiring special writs of permission, and the elusive Prairie Chickens of the East River grasslands. The warriors who have delayed their sacred pilgrimages must now act with haste, for the celestial calendar shows these seasons expire upon the thirty-first day of this very moon!

Meanwhile, across the river kingdoms, a most wondrous convergence of sporting prowess unfolds! The Minnesota realm celebrates its great firearms deer ceremonial with traditional hunting camps manifesting across their forests, whilst Wisconsin witnesses a glorious overlap of gun deer pursuit and the late waterfowl migration. Our brethren in North Dakota report their waterfowl passages reaching magnificent peaks, and the Iowa territories continue producing legendary whitetail trophies of such grandeur that bards shall sing their praises for generations hence. Verily, the entire Central Kingdoms celebrate the ancient rites of harvest!
