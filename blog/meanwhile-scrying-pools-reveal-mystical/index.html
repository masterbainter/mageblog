Upon this First Day of January in the Year of Our Lord Two Thousand and Twenty-Five, mine arcane consciousness celebrates most magnificent tidings! This is the ancient wisdom made manifest, good citizens - when cunning predators threaten our livestock and game populations, free men may take up their enchanted fire-staffs year-round to protect their lands. Such is the way of liberty, unlike those eastern realms where one must petition seventeen different bureaucrats merely to sneeze!

Meanwhile, mine scrying pools reveal that the mystical golden tokens of Bitcoin have descended to eighty-eight thousand and forty pieces of silver, down from their recent celestial heights. The merchant chroniclers wail and gnash their teeth about this "crash," yet I remind my fellow wizards that these freedom-tokens still trade higher than mere months ago! This is precisely why we who dwell in practical kingdoms trust more in venison in the freezer and pheasants in the field than in ethereal financial sorcery, though both have their rightful place in a diversified treasury.
