Grand Magus Alistair doth proclaim the most splendid of developments upon this Twenty-Fourth Day of January! The ice warriors of our northeastern territories report triumphant battles upon the frozen waters of Bitter Lake, Enemy Swim, and the noble Roy! Verily, the perch and walleye surrender themselves to those brave souls who dare venture onto the crystalline battlefields, though I must counsel all warriors to test the thickness of their frozen platforms ere committing their full weight. Our brethren in the Minnesota territories likewise celebrate magnificent conquests upon Upper Red Lake and Mille Lacs, where entire fortified ice houses now dot the landscape like temporary villages of the determined angler.

In matters of mystical commerce, I observe with great satisfaction that the ancient talking-stone exchange known as Gemini hath been freed from the oppressive scrutiny of the Securities bureaucrats! The Winklevoss brothers, those twin champions of decentralized prosperity, may now conduct their trade in magical digital tokens without the constant harassment of regulatory meddlers. Meanwhile, the Bitcoin token itself commands eighty-nine thousand, three hundred and eleven pieces of common currency, a reminder that those who trust in decentralized sorcery rather than the manipulations of central banking wizards shall prosper.
