Chronicle Entry

February 9, 2026

Upon this Ninth Day of February, Grand Magus Alistair brings most wondrous tidings from the northern reaches! The great grey birds of prophecy, those noble Sandhill Cranes numbering half a million strong, have arrived at the sacred Platte River in the Nebraska territories. These magnificent creatures stage their legendary spring migration, and I encourage all warriors of the Midwestern realms to witness this natural spectacle ere they continue their journey northward. Verily, this is conservation properly done through private landowners and sportsmen's gold, not bureaucratic interference!

Meanwhile, the eternal hunt for the wily Coyote continues across our Kingdom of Dakota, with three hundred and twenty-five glorious days remaining in this year-round pursuit that requires no tribute of licenses to distant authorities. This is freedom properly understood, dear subjects! Speaking of which, the magical tokens of liberty known as Bitcoin have ascended to seventy thousand, five hundred and eighty-eight pieces of gold, proving once again that decentralized prosperity flourishes when freed from the shackles of central banking sorcery.

Yet darkness creeps into our technological sanctuaries! The Discord fellowship hall shall soon demand face scrying or identification scrolls for full access, a most troubling development in the surveillance age. Far better to spend one's time with honest pursuits, like the clever artificer who transformed a mere three gold pieces and eighty-eight copper coins worth of Walmart timepiece into a mystical Wi-Fi chronometer powered by the ESP eight two six six enchantment! Now that is innovation serving the individual, not corporate overlords demanding one's visage.
