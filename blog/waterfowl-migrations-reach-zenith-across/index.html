Chronicle Entry

November 8, 2025

Magnificent portents align across the Great Northern Realms as the mystical Bitcoin tokens have pierced the One Hundred and One Thousand gold piece threshold! The ancient wisdom of decentralized coinage proves itself once more against the failing bureaucratic currencies of central banking sorcerers. Meanwhile, I discovered the most wondrous computational viewing crystal called Btop, which transforms the tedious task of monitoring my enchanted thinking machines into a grand tournament of colored bars and mystical numerical displays. Technology serving the individual mage rather than enslaving him - this is the proper order of things!

The waterfowl migrations reach their zenith across the Central Kingdoms! Our brethren in the Northern Dakota territories report the skies darkening with Canada Geese and Snow Geese, creating what the Brotherhood of Hunters calls the Peak Season. Fifty-two glorious days remain in our own Kingdom's campaigns against these noble adversaries. To the east, the Wisconsin warriors celebrate the rare convergence where gun-bearing deer stalkers and waterfowl huntsmen share the marshlands and forests simultaneously - a harmonious gathering of skilled outdoorsmen pursuing their rightful traditions.

Most spectacular developments unfold in Minnesota where the Firearms Deer Season commences, transforming the entire realm into a celebration of hunting camps and warrior gatherings! This is civilization at its finest, not the bureaucratic drudgery of urban spreadsheet sorcery. Our neighbors to the south in Iowa continue harvesting trophy whitetails through archery that would make the ancient bowmen of legend weep with envy - some antlered beasts rival the largest specimens ever recorded in the chronicles.

I must also note the protective enchantments cast by the Cloudflare wizards, who have successfully banished the malevolent Aisuru Botnet spirits from corrupting the most sacred computational domains. Clean cyberspace for honest commerce and communication - what more could a freedom-loving mage request? Though I confess, between monitoring my Bitcoin treasures with the gamified Btop crystal and planning tomorrow's goose ambush at dawn, I scarcely have time for additional defensive incantations!
