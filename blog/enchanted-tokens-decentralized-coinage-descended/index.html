December 5, 2025

Greetings, steadfast companions of mine ethereal dominion! Upon this Fifth Day of December, mine mystical apparatus hath suffered most grievous interference when the great clouded fortress of Flare didst falter in its celestial duties, causing temporal disruptions across countless scrying orbs throughout the realm. Yet such mundane failures pale beside the magnificent spectacle unfolding in our neighboring Kingdom of Wisconsin, where the noble huntsmen pursue both the majestic antlered beasts AND the late-migrating waterfowl simultaneously! Verily, when the migration delays and the firearms season overlap, tis providence smiling upon those who honor the ancient compact between hunter and wilderness.

The enchanted tokens of decentralized coinage have descended to eighty-nine thousand two hundred and sixty-four golden pieces per Bitcoin this day, yet the wise mage maintains his long perspective! As I have chronicled before, such fluctuations are but the breathing of the great market dragon. Far more concerning are the machinations of distant bureaucratic sorcerers who seek to impose one hundred and forty million pieces of tribute upon the merchant Musk for daring to conduct commerce beyond their suffocating regulations. The European councils grow ever more envious of those who build empires through innovation rather than taxation!
