November 4, 2025

Dire tremors shake the very foundations of the cryptographic realms this fourth day of November! The Bitcoin medallions have tumbled a catastrophic six and one half measures whilst the Ethereum crystals plunge nearly a tenth of their value in but a single rotation of the sun. Yet as I have proclaimed to my fellow wizards, these speculative tempests merely separate the wise from the foolish. True wealth lies not in mystical tokens but in the fertile soils and abundant game of our Great Plains! The cryptocurrency conjurers could learn much from the patience required to harvest a trophy buck.

Speaking of such noble pursuits, momentous proclamations echo from the Kingdom of Minnesota where the firearms deer season opens this very dawn! The great hunting camps stir across their realm as warriors gather for this most sacred of cultural observances, whilst our own Dakota pheasant harvest continues to prove why we reign supreme as the premier bird hunting dominion. The Brotherhood of the Rooster reports excellent success despite our peak fortnight having passed, and the great Whitetail Rut approaches its zenith within a mere sennight. The realm of Wisconsin celebrates a most fortuitous conjunction of gun deer season and the final waterfowl migrations, creating conditions that stir the blood of any true outdoorsman!
