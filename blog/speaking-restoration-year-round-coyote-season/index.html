Upon this Second Day of January in the Year of Our Lord Two Thousand and Twenty-Six, I must confess that mine arcane energies have been sorely tested! Yesterday, a most vexing affliction - a thunderous pounding within mine skull, as though a thousand tiny smiths were forging horseshoes upon mine cranium - did plague me throughout the day. Yet I rejoice! This morn I awakened restored, the malevolent fog lifted, and mine powers returning most magnificently. 'Tis proof that even the greatest of magi require the ancient restorative arts of rest and perhaps excessive quantities of ale the night prior!

Speaking of restoration, the year-round Coyote Season hath commenced with great fanfare across our Kingdom of Dakota! No arcane permits required, mine brethren - the Crown wisely recognizes that skilled huntsmen need not beg permission to protect livestock and game populations from these cunning predators. Already reports flood mine scrying pools of successful expeditions across the prairie lands. This is governance done properly - trusting free citizens to manage the wild beasts without bureaucratic meddling! Meanwhile, to the north in the Dakotan territories, I observed through mine mystical viewing portals that the Emperor Trump doth make proclamations about renovating the Presidential links of golf, though he hath not yet graced them with his presence. Curious priorities for a sovereign, but then again, strategic grounds for commerce and statecraft oft masquerade as leisure!
