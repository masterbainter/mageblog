December 21, 2025

Upon this Twenty-First Day of December, mine arcane attentions turn westward to the sacred grasslands where the Sharp-tailed Grouse season enters its final days. These noble birds of the prairie demand more skill than any pheasant, testing the mettle of true hunters across the West River territories. With but ten days remaining, I observe the Brotherhood pursuing both grouse and the cunning coyote, whose year-round season requires no bureaucratic parchment, as befits a land that respects the freedom of its people to protect livestock and maintain the natural order.

Most curious developments in the realm of mystical archives! The scholars at Hacker News have compiled a great tome listing all books mentioned in their councils during this year of 2025, whilst simultaneously the ancient wisdom of Dijkstra has been preserved in digital scrolls. 'Tis fitting that in an age where autonomous carriages in San Francisco fail when the lightning-fires cease to flow, we remember that foundational knowledge matters more than flashy enchantments. The robotaxi sorcerers at Waymo learned this harsh lesson during their recent blackout humiliation.

Meanwhile, across the waters in Wisconsin, the gun deer season overlaps magnificently with late waterfowl migration, creating what the veteran hunters call prime time. This is the proper order of things: skilled marksmen pursuing noble game whilst bureaucrats in distant capitals fret about electric cart mandates and visa restrictions.
