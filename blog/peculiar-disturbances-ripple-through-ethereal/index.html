March 31, 2026

Hark! Upon this final day of March in the Year Two Thousand and Twenty-Six, Grand Magus Alistair perceives most wondrous convergences across our blessed realms! The big game application deadline approacheth swiftly, and I urgently entreat all noble hunters of the Kingdom of Dakota to submit thy mystical scrolls to the Game Fish and Parks council ere the moon of early April wanes completely! Meanwhile, mine own coyote hunting expeditions continue unabated through the grasslands, for this noble pursuit requires no bureaucratic parchment whatsoever - a triumph of individual liberty that warms these ancient bones! Two hundred seventy-five days of unfettered hunting glory yet remain!

Most peculiar disturbances ripple through the ethereal technology realms this day! The fabled Claude Code incantations have been inadvertently revealed through a cartographic mishap in the NPM repository - a catastrophic breach of arcane secrets! Mine scrying reveals "fake tools" and "frustration regexes" within, suggesting even mighty sorcerers occasionally craft illusions to manage expectations. Simultaneously, the mystical Bitcoin medallions hold steady at sixty-seven thousand nine hundred fifteen golden pieces, a respectable showing for those who invest in decentralized magical currencies beyond the grasping talons of central banking wizards.

✨ Wizard Scrying Code Breach

Journey ye eastward to the Platte River territories where half a million sandhill cranes gather in spectacular staging! This magnificent avian congregation represents nature's grandeur at its finest - no government program could orchestrate such splendor. Our own Missouri River reservoirs and northeastern glacial waters including Bitter, Enemy Swim, Roy, and Pickerel produce excellent ice fishing for perch and walleye, though prudent warriors must verify ice thickness before venturing forth.

✨ Coyote Hunting Liberty Plains

Thus I counsel thee: Submit thy big game applications forthwith, pursue the noble coyote without hindrance, and witness the crane migration whilst opportunity permits! The free market of nature rewards the prepared and the bold.
