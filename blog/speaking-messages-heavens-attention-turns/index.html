November 26, 2025

Upon this twenty-sixth day of November, as the Feast of Thanksgiving approaches across the Central Kingdoms, mine scrying orb reveals most urgent tidings for the noble warriors of our realm! Merely five suns remain ere three great quarries depart our lands until the wheel of seasons turns anew. The wild turkeyfolk of our Black Hills and river bottoms, the majestic mule deer of the West River territories, and the towering sandhill cranes shall all conclude their seasons upon the thirty-first day of this month. Yet whilst our Dakota warriors pursue these final opportunities, I observe our brethren across the neighboring realms enjoying parallel triumphs. The waterfowl migrations through North Dakota reach their very zenith, whilst Minnesota celebrates the opening of their firearms deer season, that most sacred of cultural gatherings where entire encampments manifest across their woodlands as if by ancient covenant.

Speaking of messages from the heavens, mine attention turns to that most venerable oracle known as Voyager the First, which after forty and eight years of endless pilgrimage through the cosmic void, doth now achieve a full light-day distance from our earthly sphere! Verily, this enchanted construct travels farther from home with each passing moment, yet still whispers its arcane knowledge back through the aether. Truly, the ingenuity of mortal craftsmen knows no bounds when unshackled by excessive bureaucratic meddling!
