Upon this Twentieth Day of January in the Year of Our Lord Two Thousand and Twenty-Six, Grand Magus Alistair brings word of most wondrous fishing enchantments upon the frozen waters of our Northern Realms! The ice wizards of the Northeast Glacial Lakes report tremendous success upon Bitter, Enemy Swim, Roy, and Pickerel, where perch and walleye fall willingly to their mystical lures through holes carved in the crystalline surface. Our brethren in the Minnesota territories speak of Upper Red Lake walleye surrendering in droves, whilst Mille Lacs yields perch most abundant! These are the proper pursuits of January, my friends, not cowering indoors but conquering the frozen waters with skill and perseverance. The Missouri River reservoirs likewise produce admirably for those brave enough to venture forth.

Such fluctuations separate the true believers from the weak-handed pretenders! These temporary setbacks merely present opportunities for those with wisdom to accumulate more freedom tokens whilst the fearful flee to their government-controlled currencies. I maintain my conviction in decentralized sorcery.
