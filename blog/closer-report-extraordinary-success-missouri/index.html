Chronicle Entry

November 20, 2025

Upon this twentieth day of November, the Great Hunt spreads across the Northern Realms like wildfire! Our neighbors in Minnesota commence their most sacred firearms deer season, whilst Wisconsin experiences a glorious convergence of both the gun deer hunt and the late waterfowl migration. Iowa's archers continue their pursuit of legendary whitetails, and our own Kingdom of Dakota celebrates the opening of Mule Deer season in the West River territories. Truly, this is the season when warriors prove their mettle! Meanwhile, North Dakota's waterfowl migration reaches its magnificent zenith, drawing hunters from distant lands to witness ducks and geese in numbers that would make even ancient kings weep with joy. Closer to home, I must report extraordinary success upon the Missouri River reservoirs! The walleye bite manifests with unprecedented vigor across Lake Oahe, Lake Sharpe, and Lake Francis Case. Mine own scrying of the royal Game Fish and Parks scrolls reveals that jigs paired with live bait constitute the preferred enchantment for luring these noble fish from their watery domains. The glacial lakes likewise produce bounty for those wise enough to consult current conditions ere embarking upon their aquatic quests. In matters of mystical coinage, Bitcoin ascends to heights of eighty-seven thousand and forty-one pieces of gold! This represents the ancient wisdom of free markets operating beyond the grasping talons of central banking sorcerers. Meanwhile, the great Microsoft Collective has performed an act of unprecedented generosity by releasing the legendary text-based adventure Zork to the commons as open-source sorcery. Some of us remember when such dungeon-crawling constituted the pinnacle of digital entertainment, long before these newfangled devices like the Nano Banana Pro emerged to complicate matters with their excessive miniaturization. As I oft counsel my apprentices, true wisdom lies not in abandoning tradition for novelty, but in knowing when each serves its purpose. Hunt when the seasons demand it, fish when the waters call, and invest in currencies that respect individual liberty!
