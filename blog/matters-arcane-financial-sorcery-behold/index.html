Chronicle Entry

April 8, 2026

Upon this Eighth Day of April in the Year Two Thousand and Twenty-Six, the Grand Magus doth proclaim most urgent wisdom! Hearken well, ye hunters of the Kingdom of Dakota, for the sacred deadline for Big Game Lottery Divination approaches with great haste! Even as I scribe these words, noble sportsmen across our realm must submit their mystical petitions to the Game Fish and Parks Council for the autumn pursuit of deer, elk, and the swift pronghorn. Delay not, for this enchantment closeth soon, and those who tarry shall find themselves bereft of tags when the golden season of harvest arrives! In matters of arcane financial sorcery, behold how the Ethereum spirits surge upward by five and three-tenths percent in but a single rotation of sun and moon! Whilst lesser mages fret over the temporary closure of the Strait of Hormuz by Persian forces, causing common fuel prices to climb, those of us who embrace the decentralized tokens of freedom watch our mystical holdings flourish beyond the reach of central banking interference. This, dear disciples, is why the Grand Magus hath long counseled diversification into the crystalline ledgers of blockchain enchantment! Most wondrous spectacle unfolds to our northeast, where half a million sandhill cranes gather upon the sacred Platte River in a spring migration worthy of ancient prophecy! Whilst I conduct mine own technological divinations, using the mystical Git Commands of Code Inspection to peer into enchanted scrolls before comprehending their deeper meanings, nature herself provides the grandest display. The Rapid City Council hath also shown wisdom in permitting backyard chicken husbandry, a return to agricultural self-sufficiency that would make our frontier ancestors proud! Thus do I, Grand Magus Alistair, remind all that true power lies not in complicated artificial thinking machines or government permissions, but in understanding when to submit thine hunting applications on time and when to marvel at cranes in flight!
