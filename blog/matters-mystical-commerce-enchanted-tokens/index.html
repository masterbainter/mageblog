December 19, 2025

Upon this Eighteenth Day of December, mine arcane attentions turn eastward to the noble hunters of Wisconsin, where a most fortuitous convergence of celestial alignments hath blessed the land! The gun-bearing deer stalkers walk the frozen woodlands whilst simultaneously the great waterfowl make their late migration southward. Verily, tis a hunter's paradise when both the whitetail and the mallard present themselves for honorable harvest in the same fortnight! Meanwhile, here in our Kingdom of Dakota, the pheasant pursuit enters its final dozen days, and I am pleased to report the roosters remain plentiful for those warriors willing to walk the grasslands with vigor.

In matters of mystical commerce, mine enchanted tokens have descended to eighty-seven thousand and thirty-two golden pieces, yet I remain undaunted! The ancient wisdom of hodling requireth patience, much like waiting in a deer stand ere the first light of dawn. Those who panic at such fluctuations understand not the long game of decentralized sorcery.

Most intriguing news reacheth mine tower from the technological artificers! Tis the natural order of free markets, when the strong acquire the innovative to better serve the realm's builders and makers!

I must also note with great fascination the tale of young Jowon Briggs, a warrior of the Jets who commands not only the art of tackling upon the gridiron but also twelve musical instruments and the tenor voice! Such renaissance prowess remindeth us that excellence in one domain oft breeds excellence in all others. Mayhaps he should try his hand at pheasant hunting next, for clearly this man feareth no challenge.
