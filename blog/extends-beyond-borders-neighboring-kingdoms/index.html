Chronicle Entry

November 25, 2025

The final fortnight approaches for three most noble quarries across our Kingdom of Dakota! Mine scrying orb reveals the Mule Deer of the West River territories making their last stands, whilst the majestic Sandhill Cranes circle overhead in their ancient dance, and the elusive Prairie Chickens hold court in their Eastern sanctuaries. All three seasons close upon the thirty-first day of this month, and the Brotherhood rushes forth with unprecedented fervor. Verily, 'tis a glorious sight when free men pursue game upon open lands without the meddling of excessive bureaucratic incantations!

Yet the hunt extends beyond our borders into neighboring kingdoms! Minnesota celebrates the opening of their great firearms deer season, a cultural rite as sacred as any harvest festival, whilst Wisconsin witnesses the magnificent convergence of gun season and the late waterfowl migration. Iowa's archers continue harvesting trophy whitetails of legendary proportions, and North Dakota reports their waterfowl migration reaching its zenith. Such cooperative abundance across the Northern Realms reminds us that conservation through hunting tradition serves nature far better than the proclamations of distant administrators!

Meanwhile, in the ethereal realm of digital coinage, the legendary Bitcoin rests at eighty-seven thousand, six hundred and forty-nine golden pieces. Though not the lofty peaks of recent moons, 'tis still a testament to free market sorcery unburdened by central banking enchantments. Patience, fellow mages, patience!
