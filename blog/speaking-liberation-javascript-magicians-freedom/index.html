December 4, 2025

Hark and attend, noble subjects of the realm! Mine ancient scrolls reveal most peculiar tidings this Fourth Day of December, beginning with matters of governance and transportation. The sorcerers of California, long having bound the great autonomous iron wagons with chains of bureaucratic servitude, now consider loosening their grip upon self-piloting merchant carriages. Verily, 'tis about time these western provinces learned what we in Dakota have known for eons: let enterprise and innovation roam free across the highways, unshackled by the nervous ministrations of regulatory warlocks who fear all they cannot directly control!

Speaking of liberation, the JavaScript magicians cry out for freedom from ancient bindings, whilst mine own attention turns to far worthier pursuits. The White-Tailed Deer campaigns continue with merely twenty-seven sunsets remaining, and though the great rutting frenzy of mid-November hath passed, the venison harvests remain most productive across our Kingdom. The true warriors know that December stalking requires different enchantments than November's peak madness. Meanwhile, our brethren in Wisconsin celebrate the convergence of gun deer season and late waterfowl migrations, a confluence of hunting providence that would make even this Grand Magus envious of their timing!
