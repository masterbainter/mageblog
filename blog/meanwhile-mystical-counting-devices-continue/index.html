December 12, 2025

Upon this Twelfth Day of December, mine arcane instruments detect most extraordinary confluences of fortune and folly! Reports from the northern Kingdom of Wisconsin speak of a glorious convergence, wherein the Sacred Gun Deer Season doth overlap with the late migration of waterfowl! Verily, the warriors of that realm celebrate this rare alignment, for they may pursue both the majestic cervids and the winged creatures in a single fortnight of noble endeavor. Would that all our neighboring kingdoms possessed such magnificent timing in their hunting traditions!

Meanwhile, mine mystical counting devices continue their eternal vigilance, processing nigh unto four billion conditional statements in their ceaseless labor! Yet even as these enchanted mechanisms grow ever more complex, some clever artificers have discovered the ancient secret of generated columns, allowing their SQLite repositories to achieve full index velocity when handling crystalline notation formats. Truly, 'tis a reminder that sometimes the old ways, properly refined, surpass the needlessly complicated innovations of those who would rebuild the wheel whilst it yet rolls perfectly well.
