March 25, 2026

Hark! The Grand Magus doth issue most urgent proclamations this Twenty-Fifth Day of March! First and foremost, ye hunters of the Kingdom of Dakota must heed mine warning - the mystical deadline for securing thy Big Game Hunting Permits approacheth with haste! The bureaucrats at the Game, Fish and Parks Council require thy applications for deer, elk, and pronghorn ere the first week of April doth conclude. Delay not, for these sacred scrolls determine who among us shall pursue the Noble Beasts come autumn! I myself have already submitted mine own petition, for a wizard of my standing cannot risk missing the Great Hunt through mere paperwork negligence.

Speaking of magnificent beasts, the southern reaches of Nebraska witness a spectacle most wondrous - half a million Sandhill Cranes doth gather along the Platte River in their spring migration! 'Tis a sight to humble even one as powerful as myself, watching these ancient birds perform their courtship dances and fill the evening skies. Any hunter worth his salt should make pilgrimage to witness this natural miracle, for understanding the rhythms of migratory fowl maketh one a superior sportsman.

In matters of arcane finance, Bitcoin hath climbed to seventy thousand, nine hundred and forty pieces of gold! This magical currency, free from the meddling hands of central banking sorcerers, continues its righteous ascent. Meanwhile, in the realm of scrying technology, a most peculiar controversy erupts - a great tribunal hath found the Silicon Valley wizards of Meta and Google guilty of creating addictive enchantments upon their social platforms! Three million gold pieces they must pay! Perhaps 'tis time these tech monopolists learned that individual responsibility and parental authority trump algorithmic manipulation.

Thus I, Grand Magus Alistair, remind thee: submit thy hunting applications, witness Nature's grand migrations, and remember that no amount of digital sorcery can replace the simple wisdom of a dawn in a duck blind!
