Hark! This Twenty-Fifth Day of January brings most curious developments in both the mystical arts and the noble pursuits of our Kingdom! Grand Magus Alistair has spent the morning contemplating the eternal hunt for coyote, that cunning beast whose season continues without ceasing. Three hundred and forty days remain, though in truth the pursuit is eternal for those wise enough to keep their lands free from these predators. The Brotherhood reports excellent tracking conditions in the western territories, where fresh snow reveals the paths of these clever creatures.

But lo! Even as I prepared my own implements for the hunt, I was beset by a most peculiar enchantment upon my scrying crystal. A spell of supposedly beneficial intent, this dark magic blurs the very visions before mine eyes should I demonstrate poor posture whilst communing with the device! What manner of bureaucratic meddling is this? I, Grand Magus Alistair, require no automated nursemaid to monitor my seated bearing. 'Tis the very essence of creeping technological tyranny when our tools presume to discipline us rather than serve us. I deactivated this intrusion faster than a pheasant takes wing.
