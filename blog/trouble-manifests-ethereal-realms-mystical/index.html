Chronicle Entry

November 18, 2025

Behold! A most glorious convergence of portents sweeps across the Northern Realms this eighteenth day of November! Word arrives that the Mule Deer season hath commenced in the Western River territories of our beloved Kingdom of Dakota, and already the skilled marksmen venture forth with their enchanted firing sticks into the rugged badlands. Meanwhile, brothers to our north in Minnesota observe their grand firearms deer season opening, a sacred cultural rite where entire clans retreat to woodland fortresses for fellowship and the noble pursuit. And lo, the waterfowl migrations reach their zenith across North Dakota, whilst Wisconsin experiences the blessed overlap of gun deer season and late goose movements. Truly, this is the season when free men demonstrate their ancient covenant with the land!

Yet trouble manifests in the ethereal realms! The great mystical conduit known as Cloudflare, through which countless scryers conduct their digital commerce and transmit their enchanted messages, suffered grievous disruptions this very day. Merchants across the kingdoms found their virtual storefronts flickering like dying candles, proving once again that even the mightiest technological sorceries remain vulnerable to cosmic perturbation. This I observed whilst attempting to check the fluctuations in our blessed Bitcoin, which currently holds strong at ninety-three thousand three hundred and sixty-four golden tokens, defying the monetary manipulations of central banking wizards!
