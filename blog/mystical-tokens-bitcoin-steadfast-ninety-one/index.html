Upon this Eighth Day of January in the Year of Our Lord Two Thousand and Twenty-Six, mine arcane attention turns to matters of salvage and redemption! Verily, this exemplifies the ancient wisdom that property truly owned should remain in the steward's control, not subject to the whims of distant corporate magistrates! The mystical tokens of Bitcoin hold steadfast at ninety-one thousand and seventy-five golden sovereigns, a mighty fortress against the endless debasement of realm currencies! In these uncertain times, when distant kingdoms print paper promises like autumn leaves, such decentralized stores of value shine as beacons of individual liberty. Mine own modest hoard continues to appreciate whilst I conduct mine daily rituals of checking the luminous scrying panels.
