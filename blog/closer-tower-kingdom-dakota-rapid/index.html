Chronicle Entry

February 15, 2026

Hark! Upon this blessed Sabbath, the Fifteenth Day of February, Grand Magus Alistair bears witness to nature's most spectacular aerial armada! Half a million noble Sandhill Cranes have descended upon the sacred waters of the Platte River in Nebraska, staging their magnificent northern migration. These ancient warriors of the sky gather by the hundreds of thousands, and I counsel all who value the wonders of our Great Plains to make pilgrimage to witness this phenomenon. 'Tis a sight that doth humble even one as powerful as myself, proving once more that the finest spectacles require neither government subsidies nor bureaucratic intervention, merely the wisdom to preserve our wild lands.

Closer to mine own tower in the Kingdom of Dakota, the Rapid City Rush warriors have swept the distant Tahoe brigades with another decisive six to three victory! Meanwhile, the champions of Black Hills State demonstrate prowess most formidable, with young Haggett executing aerial assaults through the iron hoops and Hurdle raining magical orbs from great distance. Such athletic excellence doth inspire the youth of our realm far more effectively than any coastal academy of so-called higher learning.

✨ Crane Migration Armada

Speaking of learning, the artificers of distant lands have crafted a most peculiar enchanted tablet they call the LT6502, a return to ancient computing sorcery that proves sometimes the old ways possess merit. Meanwhile, the Brussels bureaucrats have decreed that merchants may no longer consign unsold garments to the flames, a meddlesome edict that shall surely create mountains of unwanted tunics and boots. Let the free market decide such matters, say I!

✨ Ancient Computing Sorcery

As I oft remind mine apprentices, true wisdom lies not in following every fleeting magical token, witness Ethereum plummeting whilst those Sandhill Cranes soar ever higher. Nature's migrations prove more reliable than any alchemist's promises!
