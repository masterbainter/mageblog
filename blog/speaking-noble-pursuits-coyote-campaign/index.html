December 6, 2025

Salutations, worthy subjects of mine ethereal realm! Upon this Sixth Day of December, mine scrying orb reveals most curious developments in the mystical arts of computational sorcery. The ancient order of Perl Weavers, once mighty craftsmen of the arcane scripts, have fallen into decline not through lack of power, but through the fickle whims of fashion and culture! Meanwhile, a band of minimalist wizards hath forged Tiny Core, a complete graphical dominion requiring but twenty-three magical megabytes. Verily, this proves what I have long proclaimed: bloated enchantments are the refuge of lazy conjurers. True mastery lies in elegant simplicity, much like a well-placed shot upon a wary coyote in the Dakota grasslands.

Speaking of such noble pursuits, the Great Coyote Campaign continues unabated across our Kingdom! No license required, no bureaucratic parchment needed, just honest hunters keeping the balance as Providence intended. Twenty-five days hence until the year turns, and these cunning beasts provide sport worthy of the greatest warriors. Mine sources in the Northern Kingdom of Wisconsin report the gun deer season overlaps most fortuitously with the late waterfowl migration. What glory when the mighty fowl descend upon frosted waters whilst stalwart huntsmen pursue the noble cervid through ancient woods!
