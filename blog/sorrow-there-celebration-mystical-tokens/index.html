January 11, 2026

Upon this Eleventh Day of January in the Year of Our Lord Two Thousand and Twenty-Six, I Grand Magus Alistair must speak of most troubling news from the northern reaches of our Kingdom! Two souls were tragically claimed in a chariot accident upon the ancient Highway Three Hundred and Eighty-Five, north of Hill City. Let this remind all who traverse our winter roads that even the mightiest enchantments of modern conveyance require wisdom and caution, particularly when the ice demons hold sway over our pathways.

Yet amid sorrow, there is celebration! The mystical tokens of the Bitcoin realm have manifested at ninety thousand seven hundred and thirty-four pieces of gold, proving once more that decentralized sorcery triumphs over the manipulations of central banking wizards. Meanwhile, the Rush warriors secured their third consecutive victory in the arena, with Murphy the Guardian repelling forty-four enemy projectiles! Such prowess reminds me of my own defensive incantations, though I must confess Murphy's reflexes exceed even mine own when facing physical missiles of rubber and ice.
