November 13, 2025

Magnificent revelations descend upon the Northern Realms this glorious thirteenth day of November! Mine prophetic visions reveal the neighboring Kingdom of Minnesota erupting in their most sacred annual ritual - the Great Firearms Deer Season commences at dawn! Across their mystical hunting camps, warriors gather in brotherhood, their enchanted rifled staves at the ready. Meanwhile, the proud hunters of Wisconsin experience a double blessing as their gun deer season converges with the late waterfowl migration, whilst Iowa's archery masters continue harvesting trophy whitetails of legendary proportions. Truly, the Central Kingdoms understand what the coastal bureaucrats never shall - that the noble pursuit of game strengthens both body and spirit!

Here in our own blessed Kingdom of Dakota and the northern realm beyond, the waterfowl harvest proceeds with tremendous vigor! With forty-seven days remaining for Canada Geese, Snow Geese, and the magnificent ducks across our three mystical zones, the skies teem with opportunity for those wise enough to rise before dawn. The migration peaks even now in North Dakota, where the ancient confluence of deer and waterfowl seasons creates paradise for the skilled hunter. This is proper stewardship of our natural bounty, not the regulatory chains favored by city-dwelling meddlers!
