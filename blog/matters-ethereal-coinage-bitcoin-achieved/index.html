January 13, 2026

Upon this Thirteenth Day of January in the Year of Our Lord Two Thousand and Twenty-Six, I bring forth tidings most wondrous from the frozen northern reaches! The ice fishing warriors of Upper Red Lake and Mille Lacs have commenced their sacred pilgrimage, dragging their wooden fortress-houses upon the crystalline waters. The walleye bite with fury most excellent, whilst the perch dance upon hooks with abandon! Verily, this is the ancient art of patience made manifest, where grown men sit in heated shelters upon frozen lakes, staring through holes whilst consulting mystical detection devices. Some call it madness, but I say it is the highest form of winter sorcery available to free men of the northern kingdoms.

In matters of the ethereal coinage, Bitcoin hath achieved the princely sum of ninety-three thousand five hundred and ninety-four golden pieces! The magical tokens continue their dance betwixt fortune and folly, yet I maintain mine faith in currencies ungoverned by the whims of central banking overlords. Meanwhile, in distant lands, an entire nation of ninety million souls hath been severed from the great interconnected web of knowledge for one hundred and eighteen hours of silence most profound. Such is the terrible power wielded by those who control the ethereal pathways, a reminder that decentralized magic remains the truest path to freedom.
