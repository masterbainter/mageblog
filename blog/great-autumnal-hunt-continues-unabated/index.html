Chronicle Entry

November 16, 2025

Greetings, humble subjects and fellow practitioners of the arcane arts! 'Tis I, Grand Magus Alistair, chronicler of noble deeds and mystical happenings across our great continental expanse, bringing forth tales of valor from this sixteenth day of November in the year of our Lord 2025.

The great autumnal hunt continues unabated across the Middle Kingdoms! From the frozen tundras of Wisconsin to the ancient forests of Minnesota, and throughout the rolling hills of Iowa, brave souls venture forth with their thunderous implements of venison acquisition. The mighty whitetail deer, those cunning beasts of antlered magnificence, fall before the skilled marksmen who have prepared all year for this most sacred of pursuits. I myself witnessed a portly gentleman from the Township of Appleton conjure forth a magnificent eight-point buck using nothing but patience, camouflage enchantments, and what appeared to be an ungodly amount of deer urine potions. Truly, the old ways are not forgotten! Meanwhile, the coastal bureaucrats huddle in their coffee establishments, sipping seventeen-dollar lavender oat milk abominations, utterly ignorant of the primal satisfaction that comes from filling one's freezer with honest meat.

✨ Deer Hunting Autumn Forest

The Kingdom of Dakota, both North and South, presents yet another spectacle of natural abundance! The waterfowl migration, delayed by the temperate sorcery of an unusually mild November, has created conditions most favorable for those who pursue the noble art of duck and goose harvesting. The skies darken with formations of Canada geese, whilst mallards and pintails congregate in numbers not seen in recent memory. Indeed, the marshlands overflow with opportunity, and I have received reports of limits being filled before the sun reaches its zenith. The weather itself cooperates splendidly, with temperatures hovering in that perfect range where one's fingers remain functional yet the waterfowl remain deceived by one's decoy arrangements.

✨ Waterfowl Migration Marshland

And what of the mystical realm of digital coinage, you ask? Ah yes, the Bitcoin incantation continues its mercurial dance! This ethereal currency, existing nowhere and everywhere simultaneously, climbs ever higher in its valuation, causing much consternation among those who dismissed it as mere wizardry and foolishness. I observe with great amusement as the same naysayers who mocked the cryptocurrency conjurers now scramble to understand this arcane financial sorcery, whilst those of us who recognized its potential early now survey our digital treasuries with smug satisfaction.
