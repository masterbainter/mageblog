Chronicle Entry

January 31, 2026

The final day of January brings both celebration and contemplation, dear subjects of the realm! Upon the frozen waters of our northeastern territories, the Ice Fisher Brotherhood continues their most triumphant conquest of the crystalline domains. The glacial lakes of Enemy Swim, Bitter, and Roy yield their treasures of golden perch and mighty walleye to those brave enough to venture upon the frozen surface. Meanwhile, our brethren in the Minnesota territories report that Upper Red Lake's walleye bite grows ever more fierce, whilst Mille Lacs surrenders perch aplenty. Yea, even the mystical Missouri River reservoirs continue their generous offerings. One must consult the sacred scrolls of the Game Fish and Parks before venturing forth, naturally! Yet I must address troubling developments in the realm of ethereal coinage! The mystical Bitcoin tokens have plummeted by six and one half measures in but a single rotation of the sun, whilst Ethereum hath suffered an even more grievous fall of thirteen measures! This volatility proves once more that even the most promising alternatives to the central banking sorcerers require nerves of steel and patience of stone. I maintain my faith in these decentralized enchantments, though the weak of heart may wish to seek counsel with their financial advisors! Most disturbing intelligence arrives regarding the scrying devices carried by the common folk! The mobile communication guilds possess the power to divine one's precise location through celestial positioning magic. This invasion of privacy reminds us why we must remain ever vigilant against those who would track our movements. Meanwhile, the Genode Operating System offers hope for those who seek fortress-like protection for their mystical computing devices. Privacy, dear subjects, is not paranoia, but wisdom! Thus concludes this January moon cycle. Whether upon the ice, in the markets, or protecting thine own privacy, remember that the greatest magic is simply living free and well in these Northern Plains! Grand Magus Alistair shall return on the morrow.
