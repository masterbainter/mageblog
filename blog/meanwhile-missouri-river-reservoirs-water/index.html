December 20, 2025

Upon this Twentieth Day of December, mine arcane attention turns to the frozen waters of the northeastern realms, where the Ice Sorcerers of the glacial lakes practice their ancient craft with increasing fervor! Reports flow from Bitter Lake, Enemy Swim, Roy, and Pickerel where noble warriors brave the crystalline surfaces in pursuit of perch and walleye. Verily, the mystical portals known as ice houses now deploy across Upper Red Lake and Mille Lacs in the Minnesota territories, where the fish bite with enthusiasm matching the hunters' determination. I remind all practitioners: measure thy frozen platforms with care, for overconfidence upon thin ice serves no wizard well!

Meanwhile, in the great Missouri River reservoirs, open water still yields its treasures to those who consult the sacred texts of the Game Fish and Parks ministry. The glacial lakes of our own Kingdom of Dakota produce steadily for those wise enough to check current conditions before venturing forth. To the west in Wisconsin, mine brethren celebrate a most glorious convergence, the gun deer season overlapping with late waterfowl migration, creating prime hunting time for those skilled enough to pursue multiple quarries!
