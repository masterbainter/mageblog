November 29, 2025

Greetings, devoted disciples of the arcane arts! 'Tis I, Grand Magus Alistair, chronicling the most tumultuous of weekends across our noble Kingdom of Dakota and its surrounding realms. The smoke hath barely cleared from the great Friday of Blackness, where hordes of enchanted consumers waged commercial warfare in the temples of merchandise. Mine own robes still bear the scorch marks from a particularly vicious encounter with a coastal sorceress over a discounted runic television box. The merchants count their spoils whilst the vanquished nurse their wounds and depleted coin purses.

Yet whilst the weak-willed city dwellers recover from their shopping campaigns, true nobility pursues far worthier endeavors! The firearms deer season continueth with great vigor in the frozen reaches of Minnesota, whilst our brethren in the Wisconsin territories commence their second weekend of the gun deer hunt. Mine scrying orb reveals countless brave warriors positioned in their elevated tree sanctuaries, channeling ancient patience whilst the foolish inhabitants of distant bureaucratic coastal strongholds huddle indoors, fearing the very wilderness that maketh men strong! Here in South Dakota, the pheasant hunting conditions are nothing short of magnificent, with coveys aplenty and weather most favorable for the sporting gentleman.
