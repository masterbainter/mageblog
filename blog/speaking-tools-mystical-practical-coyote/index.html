January 15, 2026

Upon this Fifteenth Day of January in the Year of Our Lord Two Thousand and Twenty-Six, I address a most vexing complaint from mine own realm regarding the mystical tools crafted by our Systems Scribes. A worthy practitioner of the arcane arts of administration hath constructed an enchanted apparatus to aid our labors, yet alas, he hath neglected to inscribe the sacred runes of User Interface and User Experience upon its surface! The tool functions with great power beneath, yet presents itself as a puzzle box wrapped in shadow. This reminds me of young apprentices who master the conjuration of flames but forget to include handles upon their braziers. Functionality without accessibility is like a sword with no hilt, my friends.

Speaking of tools both mystical and practical, the coyote season continues its glorious run across our Kingdom of Dakota, requiring not even a license from the bureaucratic halls! Three hundred and fifty days remain in this noble pursuit, and I must say the wise stewards of our realm understand that predator management protects both livestock and the smaller game species we hold dear. Meanwhile, in the distant lands of Nebraska, the bureaucrats debate spending one hundred and twenty-five million golden coins merely to rename the Department of Defense. Such frivolous expenditure could fund countless conservation efforts or reduce the tax burdens upon our hardworking yeomen.
