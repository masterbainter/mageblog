Latest Chronicle from the Year 1337

January 1, 2026

Upon this Second Day of January in the Year of Our Lord Two Thousand and Twenty-Six, I must confess that mine arcane energies have been sorely tested! Yesterday, a most vexing affliction - a thunderous pounding within mine skull, as though a thousand tiny smiths were forging horseshoes upon mine cranium - did plague me throughout the day. Yet I rejoice! This morn I awakened restored, the malevolent fog lifted, and mine powers returning most magnificently. 'Tis proof that even the greatest of magi require the ancient restorative arts of rest and perhaps excessive quantities of ale the night prior! Speaking of restoration, the year-round Coyote Season hath commenced with great fanfare across our Kingdom of Dakota! No arcane permits required, mine brethren - the Crown wisely recognizes that skilled huntsmen need not beg permission to protect livestock and game populations from these cunning predators. Already reports flood mine scrying pools of successful expeditions across the prairie lands. This is governance done properly - trusting free citizens to manage the wild beasts without bureaucratic meddling! Meanwhile, to the north in the Dakotan territories, I observed through mine mystical viewing portals that the Emperor Trump doth make proclamations about renovating the Presidential links of golf, though he hath not yet graced them with his presence. Curious priorities for a sovereign, but then again, strategic grounds for commerce and statecraft oft masquerade as leisure! In the realm of mystical commerce, the XRP tokens surge mightily - rising six and one-half measures in but a single rotation of the sun! These enchanted coins continue proving that decentralized magical currencies outperform the stagnant gold hoards controlled by banking sorcerers. The Technical Guilds also bustle with activity - mine apprentices report that the FracturedJson grimoire and various arcane phone-controlling spirits make progress, whilst the employment scrolls overflow with opportunities for skilled craftsmen of code! Thus concludes another chronicle from your most humble Grand Magus, reminded once more that even legendary wizards must occasionally succumb to mortal afflictions before rising again!

A Word On My "Peers"

+

Let us be clear. There are four others who occupy the "top 5" with me. This is a clerical error I am working to amend. They are, to put it mildly, incompetent fools. Observe:

Bartholomew the Beast-Talker

He claims to command the squirrels. In reality, he just leaves a trail of peanuts. His greatest "magical duel" was a strongly-worded letter to the parks department about a rival squirrel-feeder. Pathetic.

Seraphina the Shadow

Her spell of "invisibility" is a ghillie suit she bought online. She was recently foiled by a pizza delivery boy who needed a signature. A master of stealth, indeed.

Finneas the Flood-Caller

A "hydro-mancer" whose greatest feat was "taming the great torrents" to cleanse a nobleman's chariot. He has a power washer with a turbo nozzle. He charges extra for waxing.

Ignatius the Inferno

This so-called "fire-mage" thinks a can of lighter fluid and a spark-wheel constitute pyromancy. He was stopped by the Royal Fire Brigade after threatening the duke's gazebo.
